Notice

04 AUG

New Business Administration Course

We are excited to announce the introduction of a new Business Administration course starting this fall semester. This course aims to equip students with advanced skills in management, finance, and marketing. Enroll now to gain hands-on experience and practical knowledge from industry experts. For more information, please visit the Business Department office or our website.

02 JUL

Business Internship Opportunities

The Career Services Center is pleased to offer new internship opportunities with top-tier companies for business students. These internships provide invaluable real-world experience and networking opportunities. Interested students should submit their resumes and cover letters by September 15th. For further details, please contact the Career Services office.

08 JUN

Upcoming Business Seminar Series

The Business Department is hosting a series of seminars featuring prominent business leaders and entrepreneurs. These seminars will cover various topics, including innovation, entrepreneurship, and global business trends. The first seminar will be held on August 25th in the Main Auditorium. Students are encouraged to register early as seats are limited.

06 MAY

Business Club Meeting

The University Business Club will hold its first meeting of the semester on August 20th at 6:00 PM in Room 101. All students interested in business, entrepreneurship, and networking are welcome to attend. Join us to learn about upcoming events, guest speakers, and opportunities for professional development.

12 APR

New Research Grants for Business Students

We are pleased to announce that new research grants are available for business students working on innovative projects. These grants aim to support research in areas such as sustainable business practices, digital transformation, and economic development. Application forms can be obtained from the Research Office and must be submitted by September 10th.

09 MAR

Business Department Office Hours

The Business Department has updated its office hours to better serve students. Faculty members will be available for consultation and academic advising from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Students are encouraged to schedule appointments in advance to ensure availability. For more information, please visit the Business Department office or our website.
